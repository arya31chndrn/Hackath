Trivandrum

Kovalam

A popular beach destination with scenic views, clear waters, and great opportunities for swimming and surfing.

Padmanabhaswamy Temple

An ancient Hindu temple famous for its rich history, stunning architecture, and being one of the wealthiest temples in the world.

Kilimanoor Palace

A historical palace known for its traditional Kerala architecture and being the birthplace of artist Raja Ravi Varma.

Vizhinjam Port Harbour

A natural harbor near Trivandrum, known for its picturesque views and the upcoming deep-water port project.

Kanyakumari

A coastal town at India's southern tip, famous for its breathtaking sunrise, sunset, and the Vivekananda Rock Memorial.

Ernakulam

Hill Palace Museum

Explore the royal heritage of Kerala in this historic palace-turned-museum.

Mattancherry Palace

A beautiful blend of Kerala and Portuguese architecture showcasing history.

Kerala Folklore Museum

Immerse yourself in Kerala's rich cultural history and folk traditions.

Thattekad Bird Sanctuary

Experience the diversity of bird species in one of Kerala's finest sanctuaries.

Thrissur

Thrissur Pooram

Known as the "festival of festivals," Thrissur Pooram is a grand cultural event featuring colorful processions and traditional music.

Athirapally Falls

Dubbed the "Niagara of India," this stunning waterfall is a must-visit attraction in Thrissur.

Vadakkumnathan Temple

An ancient temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, known for its classic Kerala-style architecture.

Chettuva Backwaters

Experience the serene beauty of backwaters with lush green surroundings and houseboat cruises.

Pathanamthitta

Gavi

Scenic eco-tourism spot amidst forests and wildlife.

Perunthenaruvi Waterfalls

A stunning waterfall set in a lush landscape.

Konni Reserve Forest

A rich wildlife reserve with elephant training camps.

Aranmula Temple

A historic temple known for its cultural significance.
