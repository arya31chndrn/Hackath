Trivandrum

Kovalam

A popular beach destination with scenic views, clear waters, and great opportunities for swimming and surfing.

Padmanabhaswamy Temple

An ancient Hindu temple famous for its rich history, stunning architecture, and being one of the wealthiest temples in the world.

Kilimanoor Palace

A historical palace known for its traditional Kerala architecture and being the birthplace of artist Raja Ravi Varma.

Vizhinjam Port Harbour

A natural harbor near Trivandrum, known for its picturesque views and the upcoming deep-water port project.

Kanyakumari

A coastal town at India's southern tip, famous for its breathtaking sunrise, sunset, and the Vivekananda Rock Memorial.

Agasthyakoodam

Kerala's second-highest peak, ideal for trekking, with lush forests and rich biodiversity.
